## Revision Rights
**You get:**
*   1 content revision per video (editing changes)
*   1 caption revision per post (text changes)
**This means:**¬†If you want changes to both the video AND caption, that's fine - but each gets one round of revisions.
**Why this policy?**¬†It keeps content moving and prevents perfectionism paralysis. In 95% of cases, the first version is great.
* * *
## What If I Miss a Deadline?
**No drama.**¬†Life happens.
**If you miss Friday upload:**
*   That content shifts to next cycle
*   We keep publishing the content you DID submit
*   Just get back on track next week
**If you miss Wednesday review:**
*   Content publishes as-is
*   You already saw it, so we assume it's approved
**If this becomes a pattern (3+ weeks):**
*   We'll have a quick call to adjust your schedule
*   Maybe you need different capture days/times
*   Or temporarily reduce to 1 post/week

## Questions About the Workflow?
**"What if I'm traveling Friday?"**
Upload Thursday night. The deadline is "by Friday 7 PM" not "on Friday."
**"Can I upload content early in the week?"**
Yes! Upload as soon as you capture it. Friday is the DEADLINE, not the requirement.
**"What if I forget my JotForm link?"**
It's in every confirmation email. Bookmark it the first time you use it.
**"How do I know what to capture each week?"**
Check your ClickUp Content calendar - every assignment has specific details.
**"What happens in** [**Frame.io**](http://Frame.io)**?"**
You'll get an email with a link. Click it, watch the video, leave comments if you want changes. Super simple.
