## 6. MESSAGING FRAMEWORK

**Core Message:** "Your ideal customers are searching for you right now—in Google, on social media, on your website—but they're not finding you. That invisible business is costing you hundreds of dollars every week. We make you visible across all three places, systematically, so those phone calls and emails come to you instead of your competitors. And it works fast—within 2-3 months you'll see a reliable flood of new leads you didn't know existed."

### Key Messages to Emphasize (Pull These Into Everything):

1. **The Opportunity Cost:** "100 people monthly might be searching for your service in your area. If you're not showing up, those calls are going to your competitors. That's $50K+ in lost revenue annually—more than our entire annual investment."

2. **The Simplicity of the Solution:** "This isn't complicated or expensive. We give Google what Google wants (complete profiles, reviews, quality content). We give customers what they want (authentic photos and videos proving you're legitimate). That gives you what you want (phone calls from people who are already looking). It's $550-$950/month, and it pays for itself in the first 3 months."

3. **The Speed:** "You'll see results faster than you think—often within 30 days. First customer calls and says 'I found you on Google.' Your phone starts ringing. That's when you know this works."

4. **We Handle the Heavy Lifting:** "You capture 10-15% (photos and videos of your team, your work, your process). We handle 85%—strategy, editing, posting, optimization, review management, ensuring authenticity. Your only commitment is a 20-minute meeting every two weeks."

5. **This Isn't "Marketing"—It's Results:** "We're not a marketing agency selling you services. We're a results service. You pay for the outcome: more qualified phone calls, higher close rates, more revenue. Everything we do is built backward from that one goal."

### Tone & Voice Characteristics:

- **Confident but not pushy:** You speak from experience without lecturing. You explain why things matter without talking down to them. You know this works because you've seen it work repeatedly. You use phrases like "Here's what I've found..." and "The reality is..." not "You need to..." or "You should..."

- **Practical and no-BS:** You cut through the noise of marketing hype. You're honest about what works and what doesn't. You acknowledge their skepticism because you've heard it a thousand times and proven it wrong a thousand times. You don't oversell; you show proof.

- **Education-focused:** Your goal is helping them understand where their business actually is (invisible, losing leads) vs. where it could be (visible, flooded with leads). You're not trying to convince them to buy marketing; you're trying to help them see what they're already losing.

- **Service-oriented:** You talk about working *with* them, not *for* them. You acknowledge that most business owners are in a frenzy and can't add more to their plate—so you make it easy. You're solving the real friction (time and complexity), not asking them to solve it themselves.

### Avoid:

- Corporate marketing speak ("leverage synergies," "growth hacking," "maximize your brand potential")
- Vague promises ("We'll help you grow" without specifics)
- Talking about yourself instead of their results (focus on their phone ringing, not your process)
- Treating them as if they're dumb or need convincing (they know they need this; they're just scared)

### Channel-Specific Guidance:

**Social Media Posts:**
- Lead with the problem or opportunity they don't realize exists ("100 people searching for you monthly... are they finding you?")
- Show before/after of visibility (what Google looked like before vs. now)
- Use client results as proof ("One client went 1-2 calls/month to 3-4 calls/week in 90 days")
- Tone: conversational, direct, no fluff. Use "we," "your," "here's what happens"
- CTAs: "See if you're showing up" (free audit), "Let's talk about your visibility" (consultation), "Ready to test this?" (free trial)

**Website/Sales Pages:**
- Open with the problem and opportunity cost ("Your competitors are getting calls that should be yours")
- Show the specific transformation (1-2 calls/month → 3-4 calls/week)
- Address objections upfront (price, time commitment, "will this really work?")
- Emphasize the free trial as the risk-removal tool
- Focus on their relief when it works, not your features

**Sales Conversations:**
- Start by discovering where they think they're invisible
- Show them what they could capture if they were visible
- Acknowledge their skepticism and prove them wrong with examples
- Emphasize that you remove the burden from them (free trial proves this)
- Close with: "Let's test this for 30 days so you can see for yourself"
